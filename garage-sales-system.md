# MRM Garage & Shop Sales System

## Core Offer

- Garages and shops for buyers in Joplin, Missouri and Northwest Arkansas
- Company installation handled for the customer
- Financing available to avoid a large upfront payment
- Average building ticket: about $12,000
- Anchor example: a $10,000 building financed for 60 months runs about $465 per month

## Lead Positioning

- Use monthly payment language before full price language
- Safe phrasing:
  - Payments starting around $400/month
  - Most buildings run about $400 to $600/month depending on size and options
  - Financing available with fast quote follow-up
- Avoid opening with one hard payment on every ad unless the size is clearly specified

## Automation Workflow

### Workflow 1: New Lead

- Trigger: Facebook lead form, website form, or manual entry
- Send immediately: "Hey this is Mike with garages and shops. Are you looking for a garage or a full shop?"
- Wait 2 minutes
- If no reply: "Just checking. Are you looking for a garage or something bigger like a shop?"

### Workflow 2: Qualification

- Trigger: customer replies
- Ask size
- Ask city
- Ask use
- Send: "Most of our buildings run somewhere in the $400 to $600 per month range depending on size and options. Want me to get you an exact quote?"
- Apply tags: `Qualified Lead`, `Financing`

### Workflow 3: Quote Push

- Trigger: lead says yes or shows clear interest
- Send: "Awesome. I will get that put together for you and send pricing shortly."
- Move pipeline stage to `Quote Sent` after quote is delivered

### Workflow 4: Post-Quote Follow-Up

- 2 hours later: "Just wanted to make sure you saw that. Any questions on it?"
- Day 1: "We have install openings coming up. Want me to check availability for you?"
- Day 3: "Most people are using financing to get started. Want me to break down options?"
- Day 5: "Still interested in getting that garage or shop set up?"
- Day 7: "Want me to hold a spot while we finalize the details?"

### Workflow 5: Missed Call Text Back

- Trigger: missed call
- Send: "Sorry I missed your call. This is Mike with garages and shops. What can I help you with?"

## Daily Execution

- Run 2 to 3 Facebook ads with different hooks
- Post 4 to 6 Marketplace listings across the city rotation
- Reply to every lead within 5 minutes when possible
- Send quotes the same day
- Follow up on all hot leads before the day ends
